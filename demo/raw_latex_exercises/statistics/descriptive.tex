\fieldname{feedbackText}
\[
s^2 = \frac{\sum_i f_i (x_i-\bar x)^2}{N-1} =\frac{{fmsqt}}{99} \approx {var}
\]
If the sample variance is $s^2 \approx {var}$ then the sample standard deviation is $s \approx \sqrt{{var}} \approx {solD}$
